Hey Educators!
Are you ready to dive into the world of remote teaching like a pro? Here are 10 awesome tips to make your online teaching journey smooth sailing:
1. Lesson Plans Go Digital!
Take those lesson plans you've poured your heart into & give them an online makeover! Tweak them a bit, sprinkle some digital magic, & voilà! You're ready to roll. Consistency is key, so find a groove that works for you & your students.
2. Software Safari!
Whether it's Google Classroom, Microsoft Teams, or Moodle, find your digital sidekick to collaborate with your students seamlessly.
3. Online Office Hours!
Keep your virtual door open during your regular teaching hours, & don't forget to schedule some extra time for those special student moments.
4. Virtual Community Builders!
Foster that classroom camaraderie online! Your students miss each other more than they'll admit, so set up platforms for them to connect & collaborate.
5. Lights, Camera, Action!
Pre-record some lessons to save time & ensure your students have access whenever they need it. Bonus: Record live sessions for encore performances!
